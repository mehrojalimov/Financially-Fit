Meet the Authors of Financially Fit

Mehroj Alimov, Savit Tumuluri, Bahodir Jumaboyev, Megan Ehrnfeldt


We are first-year computer science students at Drexel University. In an effort to help other college students and everyone in general we have developed this website to help people control their spending urges and manage their spending habits. Our website allows you to input your monthly income then see the perfect breakdown of spending on your needs, wants and necessities for that month. Also, you can input your spending for the month and see your specific breakdown in those categories.
