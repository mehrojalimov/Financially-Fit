If you are spending too much on food in a month. These are simple suggestions to cut down your spending on food.


Create a Grocery Budget: Create and stick to a budget and list when going to the grocery store.

Plan your Meals: At the beginning of each week plan your meals and stick to them.

Use Coupons: Cut down on costs by using coupons.

Make More Meals at Home: Save money by avoiding going out for food.

Grow a Kitchen Garden:Save money from buying produce.

Regrow Your Veggies: Save money by growing/regrowing your own vegtables.

Raise Chickens for Eggs: Avoid the cost of eggs by raising chickens.

Cut Back on Food Waste: By planning meals, you can cut back on creating excess food and that food becoming wasteful.

Go Vegetarian: Save money by cutting out meat products.

But in-season Produce: In-season produce is cheaper than out of season produce.

Get Cash Back: Use credit cards by high cash back rates on groceries.

Cut the Excess Weight on Produce: For produce that is bough by the weight cut the weight down by removing parts that you will not eat like stems.

Stockpile: Buying in bulk is cheaper than individual products.

Haggle for Prices: It doesn't hurt to try to haggle to save money especially on produce products.

Store Your Food Properly: Storing food properly will make to last longer.

Take Home Leftovers: Get an extra meal by taking home leftovers.

If you are spending too much on food in a month. These are simple suggestions to cut down your spending on entertainment.

Find Cheaper Alternatives to Cable or Satelite: Watch channels that are available on your regualr TV.

Cancel Unneeded Magazine, Newpaper, or Online Subscriptions: If you are no longer using these subscriptions, delete them.

Reduce Gym or Club Membership Fees: If these membership are rarely used , cancel them. If you use them rarely, find cheaper options.

Reduce Spending on Electronics and Toys: Avoid buying the coolest, new item that just got realesed.

Reduce your Cell Phone Bill:Don't pay for features and service that you do not use on a regular basis.

Reduce your Internet Bill: Save money by reducing the speed of your internet.

Spend less for Birthdays, Weddings, and other Gift Giving: Reduce the amount that you spend on such occasions.

Reduce Personal Spending Money: Decrease your expenses on person spending. An example would be to skip you self care day this month.

Spend Less on Movies: Reduce the amount you spend on going out to the movies by watching movies at home.

Save Money on Vacations Do a stay-cation and find cheap fun things to do close to home.

Save Money on Christmas Shopping Cut down the amount of presents you give or combine gifts among people you are giving to.
